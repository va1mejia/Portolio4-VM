Inside Sorority Life

This project is about the experiences and ideas of being in a sorority and what it looks like. It gives in detail definitions that are common in Greek Life, specifically sororities. It includes interviews, a time line, photo gallery, and much more. This assignment put together everything that I have learned the entire semester.
